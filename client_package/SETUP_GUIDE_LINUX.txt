Career Copilot Premium — Linux Setup Guide
==========================================

1. Open terminal in this folder
2. Run: chmod +x install_linux.sh START_PREMIUM.sh
3. Run: ./install_linux.sh
4. Add MISTRAL_API_KEY to .env
5. Run: ./START_PREMIUM.sh
6. Browser opens to http://127.0.0.1:5000
7. Press F2 during interviews; F3 to hide/show overlay

Audio for calls:
- PulseAudio loopback: pactl load-module module-loopback
- The installer runs this automatically when possible

Get free Mistral key: https://console.mistral.ai/
