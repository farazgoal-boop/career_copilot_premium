1. Open the Mac folder in Terminal.
2. Run: chmod +x install_mac.sh START_PREMIUM.sh
3. Run: ./install_mac.sh (installs Python, BlackHole audio, and dependencies).
4. Add your Mistral API key to .env or enter it in the first-run setup window.
5. Run: ./START_PREMIUM.sh — then press F2 to listen during interviews.
